Web.Comp

Authoring of webpages as problems for annual Web Design competition for high school students as lead author over 3 years while working at Grok Learning.

Visit the website

Description

The challenge: Create a sequence of simple but well-designed webpages as learning examples to teach students web design, HTML, and CSS. Concepts are introduced over 4 weeks, with the final week being a CSS Zen Garden style design tournament.

My role: In collaboration with other team members, I was responsible for writing and designing the webpages that students would then code, as well as authoring the course. New problems, 32-40 webpages, had to be written each year, and I led this effort for three years.
