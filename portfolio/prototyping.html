Building Functional Prototypes

2019 – 2020: I was originally hired as a Functional Prototyper at Qwilr and moved into a broader role as a Product Designer after six months. In both roles, I prototyped my own and other designers’ ideas so we could quickly validate them with both online user testers and customers earlier in the design process.

Description

Qwilr gives everyone the power to create beautiful web documents. I designed and prototyped “widgets” to extend the power and flexibility of the Qwilr editor, while maintaining a simple “lego-like” experience for the user.
