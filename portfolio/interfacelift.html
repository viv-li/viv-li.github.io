Learning Interface Redesign

2017: Redesign of Grok Learning platform's learning interface as project lead while working at Grok Learning.

Visit the website

Description

The challenge: The learning interface was originally designed as a 3-panel desktop layout with only high school students in mind. As we grew, the platform needed to serve younger students, who needed a friendlier interface, and university courses, who wanted longer courses.

The redesign therefore needed to make the interface layout more configurable, tablet-friendly, improve navigation for longer courses, and be flexible enough for future growth with embedded content.

My role: I led and conducted the bulk of the redesign process over 6 months, from initial scoping, to iterative UI/UX design working with another designer, to implementation and testing. I carried out the front-end development, working closely with our CTO who made backend changes.
