HackTreks

2018: Designed and developed a WordPress website for travel company HackTreks as a freelance web developer .

Description

The challenge: New company HackTreks needed a website to attract customers to their 3-month remote work and travel programs, receive applications and enquiries, and host their blog. They also needed a site that could be easily updated and maintained by non-programmers.

My role: I worked with the founders to write the website copy, and then designed and built a custom WordPress theme for the website using the Genesis Framework, as SEO is important to HackTreks.
