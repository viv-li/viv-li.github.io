Australian Computing Academy

Designed and developed the Australian Computing Academy website when it launched in July 2017.

Visit the website

Description

The challenge: The Australian Computing Academy needed a website for their launch. The website needed to inform teachers about the Digital Technologies curriculum, provide access to the DT Challenges being developed for them, and allow teachers to view and book workshops.

My role: The timeline for the project was very tight, with the design and development work on this website being completed within 2 weeks. I completed the brand design, and then designed and implemented the static website, which was built with Bootstrap, Less and vanilla JS.
